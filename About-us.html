WHY WE DO WHAT WE DO  

A Living Legacy of Giving 
Our mission is to give back, pass-on, transmit acquired wisdom from each of our 30+ years business lessons. We seek to ignite, to accompany and to be active contributors and not just consumers in this world.
 
Creating Jobs, Giving back to the Ecosystem 
We are entrepreneurial team builders by nature and believe in the spirit of collaboration and inclusion. As we provide individualized solutions our spark comes from building human connectivity.

HOW WE DO IT 

Integrity and Trust 
We listen in order to understand how to assess your needs and strategic plans and to “shape the engagement ” with you as we facilitate & educate you through the lifecycle. 
 
Benevolence, Humility and Respect
Our foundation is humility, not telling you what to do but exploring possibilities with you. We've most likely just been around for a bit longer, learned by mistakes along the way and are deeply committed to accompanying and passing-on our hard earned life insights. 
 
Right Effort / Right-Pricing 
We are committed to providing a customised approach where resources, effort and technologies are mutually agreed up to and understood. Our pricing & billing is transparent and you can use our internal Project Evaluation and Costing Tool (insert link) to run your own numbers.
We pledge to maximize your time and investment with the intention of "working ourselves out of a job" rather than becoming ingrained within your organization. Flexible pricing for your mission critical requirements is our mantra.  
 
Small Team 
As experienced technology & business professionals , rather than stay in the big global corporate world, we are building a small entity to accompany other small and mid-sized companies in your mission critical growth requirements. One of us will actively manage your projects and guide our consultants.
